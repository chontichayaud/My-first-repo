Postpartum Climbing

Climbing is a hobby that my partner and I enjoy. we have been climbing for years before I got pregnant. Now, I am a mum of 1 year old boy.

Motherhood climber

After give birth with c-section. I started to climb when my baby was 2 month, most of sites recommemed it's best to wait untill you had 6-8 weeks postnatal check before returning to your pre-pregnancy levels of exercise.

Climbing with newborn

Climbing with with my baby is the best thing! And of couse, I can't do it without my partner.

I started to climb on small grades, V0 - V2 for weeks for gain muscles back and always climb down. For the baby, buggy is essential.
